About Me

I am a seasoned UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer with over 10 years of experience in designing and developing websites. My expertise lies in managing the front-end development of client sites using technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, Angular (versions 13/14/15), Node.js, JavaScript, Unit test case and Bootstrap, SCSS, SASS, LESS.

I have a proven track record of transforming UX designs into functional web pages, leveraging tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Invision, and Adobe Photoshop. My development process incorporates modern practices such as Test-Driven Development (TDD), unit testing, and continuous integration/deployment (CI/CD) with Jenkins.

In addition to my proficiency with CSS preprocessors like SCSS and LESS, I have experience using Gulp as a task runner and Git for version control.

Throughout my career, I have developed over a hundred projects for renowned brands, including Jaquar Lighting, Medanta Hospital, Mu Sigma, Edelweiss, Tata Capital, Quick Heal, Future Generali, Navia, Get Vokl, News Cycle, Jobdroid, Kotak Education, and Mahindra, Artemis Health, Surge Learning.

Currently, I am working with ValueLabs on a project in the healthcare sector. As part of my continuous professional development, I have recently started working with Docker and Kubernetes, and I am currently undergoing training for these technologies.

My experience spans across B2B, service-based, and product-based companies, showcasing my versatility and adaptability in different work environments. I am passionate about creating user-centric designs and developing efficient, high-quality front-end solutions.
